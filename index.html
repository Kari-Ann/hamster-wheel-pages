About the Game

The rat race got a board game.

60 hours. 8 locations.

Every turn is one week. Allocate 40 weekday hours and 20 weekend hours across 8 locations — the Job Center, University, Bank, Market, Clothing Store, Pawn Shop, Gym, and your Apartment. Every hour you spend is a trade-off.

Turn-based strategy.

No time pressure — play at your own pace. One turn takes 2–5 minutes, making it perfect for commutes, breaks, or late-night sessions. Undo mistakes. Save and resume anytime.

The chaos of 2026.

Tariff announcements tank your grocery budget. Crypto pumps and dumps. The Fed raises rates. Your landlord raises the rent. Satirical weekly news events create real economic pressure — the same strategy that worked last week might not work next week.

Support

Need help?

How do I save my game?
Your progress saves automatically on your device. Just close the app and come back — everything will be where you left it.
How do I restore my purchase?
Go to the game's Settings screen and tap "Restore Purchases". Your purchase is tied to your Apple ID, so it will restore on any device signed in with the same account.
Is there multiplayer?
Yes — Hot Seat mode lets 2 players take turns on the same device. Pass the phone back and forth.
Do you collect any data?
No. The game stores save data locally on your device only. No accounts, no tracking, no ads. See the Privacy Policy for full details.
What is the free trial?
The first 7 weeks of gameplay are completely free — full access to all game mechanics, all locations, and all rivals. No credit card required, no restrictions. After week 7, a one-time $5.99 unlock continues the game forever.
